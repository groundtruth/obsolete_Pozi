Meet Your Organisation’s New Communications Platform - Your GIS


Your organisation works better when it has clear communication between those who maintain its valuable geographic information and those who require timely access to it.

Pozi is the online GIS platform that connects your existing GIS data with all your internal, remote and field staff, emergency personnel, contractors, as well as the public.

  • publish information to users across your organisation and beyond
  • capture and retrieve information on smartphones and tablets in the field
  • simplify your desktop GIS workspaces using a web-powered base map and much more...

Pozi turns your GIS into a communications platform for your whole organisation.

Use your GIS to communicate more effectively with field staff, public, remote offices, contractors and emergency personal. Pozi is the online GIS platform that web-enables your GIS data, delivering vital information to those who most need it, putting everyone on the same page.

Your Web GIS


Pozi Explorer’s simple user interface gives a wide range of users access to a wealth of information, automatically synchronised from your systems. The easiest search function of any GIS and intuitive navigation enables staff and public to quickly and easily find the information they need. No training required.

Your Mobile GIS


Empower your field staff to rapidly retrieve and capture information from any iPhone, iPad or Android device with Pozi Mobile. Your field staff record observations directly from their device to the Pozi server, where they’re available in real time to users across your organisation..

Your Data, Your Way


Extend your GIS’s capabilities beyond the limitations of traditional GIS software with Pozi Web Services: embeddable maps for your website, preconfigured base maps, publishing your data as WMS/WFS/GeoRSS, address validation, PDF feature reports, and 3D visualisation through Google Earth.

Sign up to Pozi to instantly expand your GIS’s capacity to respond to meet your organisation’s growing needs. Contact Groundtruth to find out how your GIS can fill the communication gap in your organisation.
