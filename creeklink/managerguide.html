CREATE CREEK SEGMENTS

Close

If you choose the "Create a new feature" icon you can create a new feature by clicking once on the map where you would like your feature to begin then click once again to change the direction of the connected line. You may change direction as many times as you need or double click to end the feature. Note that intermediate anchors appear whenever an anchor is moved for the first time. These anchors, although initially dimmed, have all the functionality of the original anchors.

A panel now emerges where you'll need to enter a label name in the label field and enter a management name in the management field. Then click save to save your work.

EDIT CREEK SEGMENTS

Close

If you choose the "Edit existing feature" icon you can edit an existing feature by clicking on the feature you would like to modify. You then click the edit button on the bottom left of the emerging panel. You can then move the anchor points in order to change the shape, direction or length of the feature. You can also rename the label or management fields.
